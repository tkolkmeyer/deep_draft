What's an envoi?

An envoi is a final message in any poem. It's not a term that is specific to the sestina.
You can think of it as the post script of a letter, an opportunity to speak directly to the reader, or to the subject of the poem.
It's optional! Your sestina will still qualify as a sestina without the envoi.
If you do choose to create an envoi in your sestina, you'll be creating three final lines with the first and sixth end words in line one, the fourth and third end words in line two, and the fifth and second end words in line three.
The reasoning for this is so each line adds to seven.
